Simple Slideshow – Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now()->toDateString() }}

This app, Simple Slideshow, is developed and maintained by the app developer (“we”, “us” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Data we access

The app may request access to the following on your device:

  • Photos and media: to allow you to select images from your gallery and display them in a slideshow.
  • Camera: in the future, we may allow you to take photos directly from within the app to include in your slideshow.

2. How your data is used

  • All selected or captured images are used only to show slideshows on your device.
  • We do not upload your images to any server.
  • We do not share your images or personal data with third parties.

3. Data storage

The app does not create online accounts and does not store your personal data on remote servers. Any information used by the app remains on your device and is managed by your operating system.

4. Third-party services

The app currently does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

5. Children’s privacy

Simple Slideshow is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the email listed on the app’s Google Play Store page.
