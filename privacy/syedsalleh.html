Privacy Policy – Syed Salleh Family App

Last updated: 25 November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Syed Salleh Family App (“we”, “our”, “us”, or “the app”) handles personal information belonging to invited family members who use the application. This app is strictly private, intended only for members of the Syed Salleh family, and is not available to the general public.

By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The app may collect, display, or store the following types of information:

1.1 Family Profile Information

This may include:

  • Names
  • Profile photos or family photos
  • Birthdates
  • Relationship information (for example, parent/child, spouse, sibling)
  • Contact details that you choose to provide
  • Additional notes or family-related information you voluntarily enter

This information is entered manually by family members and is intended to be visible only to authorized users of the app.

1.2 User-Provided Media

If the app allows uploading or selecting photos, it may request access to:

  • Photos and images stored on your device
  • Your device’s media library or storage

Only the photos you explicitly choose inside the app are used for app features such as displaying profiles or slideshows.

1.3 Camera Access (If Enabled)

If camera permission is requested, it is used only to allow you to capture photos directly inside the app (for example, to update a profile picture or add a family photo). Captured photos are treated the same way as other user-selected images and are not uploaded to any external service unless clearly stated by a feature you use.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the app’s family-related features, such as:

  • Displaying family profiles and relationships
  • Showing photos or media related to family members
  • Allowing you to add, edit, or view family information

We do not use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Analytics or tracking outside the family app context
  • Selling or sharing your data with third parties

3. Data Storage and Security

Family data used by this app may be stored locally on your device and/or on a private server controlled by the developer. We aim to use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, such as:

  • Using secure connections (for example, HTTPS) where applicable
  • Restricting access to the database or backend (if any) to authorized users only
  • Implementing authentication to prevent unauthorized access

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers (outside the family context)
  • Other third-party companies

Your information is intended only for use within the family app and for the benefit of invited family members. We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Permissions Used by the App

Depending on the features enabled, the app may request the following permissions:

5.1 Photos / Media / Files

Used only to let you select images from your device or display media inside the app. We do not access or use your photos without your action and consent inside the app.

5.2 Camera (if requested)

Used only to capture photos directly inside the app for purposes such as profile photos or family images. Camera access is never used for background recording or tracking.

5.3 Internet Access

If the app connects to a backend server or syncs data, internet access is required to send and receive that data securely.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app may contain profiles and information about children as part of the family tree or family records. Use of the app is intended for invited family members, and if a child uses the app, it should be under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

7. Your Rights and Choices

As a family member and user of this app, you may request:

  • Correction or update of your information
  • Removal of your profile or certain data from the app
  • Clarification about how your information is stored or used

To make a request, please contact the developer using the contact details below.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the app is in use by the family. If the app is discontinued or if you request deletion of your data, we will take reasonable steps to remove or anonymize your information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective when posted on this page, with the “Last updated” date revised accordingly.
